How do I resolve an issue where Duo Mobile fails to manually reconnect accounts?

ISSUE
An issue affecting version 4.3.0.21 of Duo Mobile for iOS could result in the app crashing when tapping "Reconnect" for an account in a disconnected state. Version 4.3.0.21 of Duo Mobile began rolling out to users on November 22, 2021 before being paused.

This issue was resolved in version 4.4.0 of Duo Mobile for iOS.

RESOLUTION
Users should update to version 4.4.0 or later.

CAUSE
This issue is related to a bug in Duo Mobile version 4.3.0.21 for iOS.
